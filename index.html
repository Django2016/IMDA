A Restaurant Dining Application.

The idea behind this project is for a restaurant or a dining body to allow its customer to download an application that will allow them to order their food in a timely fashion so that as soon as they set foot in the restaurant the food is served on the table ready. It should provide a quick and easy way to also pay for your dinner no wallet necessary, track your journey to the restaurant as well as notifications or alerts throughout the entire process. Provide a friendly interface to allow easy navigation and should give directions how to get to the restaurant. Developed with ASP.Net and MVC 5.

Football club application

The main aim of the system was for a football club to keep track of their teams upcoming matches.

I was heavily involved in the development of a Football Club System. This project was 12 weeks long. Our team consisted of three members. Each of us worked on the system. The system was developed in Java with a SQL database while using Java Swing GUI's. The main aim of the system was for a football club to keep track of their teams upcoming matches, previous results, league tables, players and training sessions. Players were also given the ability to pay their weekly fees through the system by credit card which was checked through our model.
